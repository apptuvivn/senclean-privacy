# Privacy Policy - SenClean

**Last updated: April 2026**

SenClean ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Data Collection
- SenClean does **NOT** collect any personal information
- All data processing happens entirely on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party

## 2. Storage Access
- The app requires storage permission to scan and clean junk files
- Files are only deleted with your explicit permission
- The Privacy Vault feature stores hidden files locally on your device

## 3. Advertisements
- We display ads through Google AdMob
- Google may collect anonymous device identifiers for ad personalization
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

## 4. Third-Party Services
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage statistics)
- Firebase Remote Config (app configuration)

## 5. Children's Privacy
- SenClean is not intended for children under 13
